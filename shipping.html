South Africa

Nature’s Veil will charge a flat rate of R83 as from 1 May for all parcels. This excludes deliveries to outlying areas, special deliveries to farms or delivery addresses on gravel roads. Deliveries to these areas will not be possible on the store and a PEP/PostNet option needs to be selected.

  • PEP/ PostNet store delivery is R69
  • Orders of R6 000+ will qualify for free delivery

Namibia

  • Windhoek delivery is R 60
  • Namibia (Outside Windhoek) = R 132
  • Orders of R6 000+ will qualify for free delivery

Delivery takes 2-3 working days to main centres and 4-5 working days to regional centres and outlying areas.
Delivery takes 4-5 working days for PEP Store delivery.
Delivery takes 2-3 working days for PostNet store deliveries to main centres and 3-5 working days for PostNet deliveries to regional centres.
